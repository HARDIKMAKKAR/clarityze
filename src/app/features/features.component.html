How it solves eye problems?


VisuoPrime Home Edition offers a wide range of therapies.
The doctor creates an exclusive configuration for the patient,
based on the degree of vision problems.

Amblyopia: Monocular

Monocular therapies: Good eye of the patient is occluded and the therapy is done for the affected eye.

Amblyopia: MFBF

Monocular fixation in binocular field: Patient wears red-blue glasses and contents of the therapy are projected in such a way that the patient views the target with the amblyopic eye and the background with the fellow eye.

Anti-Suppression

This procedure is designed in such a way that the patient can complete the task only when he uses both eyes. This is to eliminate suppression.

Accommodation

Here the patient's capacity to adjust the focusing abilities of his eyes is trained.

Vergence Problems

The patient has the problem of converging or diverging the eyes appropriately, which may result in eyestrain, headaches, double vision, etc.

Depth Perception

The patient experiences problems in viewing space in 3D, affecting various activities and limiting work.
