Screen Height

Your web-browser is zoomed

We'll need to correct this before we can properly calibrate your device. We need the zoom level to be neutral, meaning neither zoomed-in nor zoomed-out.

Press Ctrl-Zero to reset the zoom level.

Most of the time we automatically detect when the zoom level has been reset, but you can also manually confirm that the zoom level has been reset.

Working Distance


How far do you sit from the screen? Measure the distance from your face to the screen. If you don't have a measuring tape, you may omit this step.
